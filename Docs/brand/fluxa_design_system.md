# Fluxa Design System

## Brand
- Name: `Fluxa`
- Tagline: `Suas finanças. No fluxo certo.`
- App package: `com.nagcode.fluxa`
- Deep link scheme: `fluxa://`

## Color Tokens
- Primary: `#2563EB`
- Primary Hover: `#1D4ED8`
- Secondary: `#3B82F6`
- Success: `#22C55E`
- Danger: `#EF4444`
- Background: `#F8FAFC`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#0F172A`
- Secondary Text: `#64748B`

## Typography
- Product recommendation: `Manrope`, `Sora`, or `Plus Jakarta Sans`
- Current app base: system typography with weighted hierarchy
- Headline: high contrast, compact, no decorative styling
- Body: neutral and readable, low-noise UI copy

## Shape
- Small radius: `12`
- Medium radius: `18`
- Large radius: `24`
- Extra large radius: `28`
- Pill radius: `999`

## Elevation
- Cards use one soft atmospheric shadow with low alpha
- Avoid layered shadows or noisy neumorphism

## Usage Notes
- Keep `assets/brand/source` restricted to editable official masters only.
- Do not use charts, wallets, or currency glyphs as the primary identity.
- The symbol master is the source for app icon, splash, and standalone brand usage.
- The board master is the source for presentation, lockup, and app identification panels.
- The light lockup is preserved as a generated export for marketing and presentation contexts.
- Prefer the symbol by itself for app icon and splash.
- Use the lockup only where the wordmark has room to breathe.
- Keep the symbol on white or navy backgrounds.
